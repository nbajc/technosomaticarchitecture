FIG I. THE DIGITAL PROPHYLACTIC

An autopsy on the
digital interface.

"Through the lens of the female gaze, I perform an autopsy on the digital interface—treating it not as a tool for optimization, but as a site of visceral, structural friction."

01. MANIFESTO

The Digital Prophylactic

Modern architecture of the digital realm functions as a technological membrane. The Digital Prophylactic is the primary infrastructure of contemporary intimacy—a sterile sanctuary that provides the architecture for connection while ensuring the subject remains permanently, physically quarantined.

FIG II. THE ECONOMY OF THE PHANTOM

The Economy of the Phantom

I expose the market of contemporary desire: Swipe. Trade pics. Negotiate worth. In this economy, chemistry is replaced by convenience and intimacy by mere access. Everyone is horny; no one is human.

02. TAXONOMY

I. The Phantom Emotion

(The Pseudo-Partner)

I document the 'teleportation' of intimacy between individuals who have spent years building an alternate reality through screens. In this space, the partner is not a 'loved one' in the traditional sense, but a Phantom—a vivid, persistent projection of shared history and digital habit.

FIG III. PHANTOM EMOTION

II. The Digital Void

(The Sterile Sanctuary)

I define the digital interface as a Void—a non-place where these persistent relationships are housed. This space is a 'dead end' where the illusion of the other terminates. For the digital couple, the Void is their only shared geography; it is a sterile sanctuary engineered for a connection that remains permanently physically quarantined.

FIG IV. THE VOID

III. The Biological Glitch

(Auto-Erotic Devolution)

The climax of this displacement is the Biological Glitch. The Technosomatic Homunculus—the brain’s map of the other—fuses with the digital signal, triggering a profound physiological climax in a sensory vacuum. It is an auto-erotic devolution where the 'relationship' eventually collapses into the self.

FIG V. BIOLOGICAL GLITCH
03. STATEMENT

"Technosomatic Architecture utilizes the female gaze as a clinical lens."

Moving away from the voyeuristic "God’s-eye view" of traditional representation, my work investigates the Synthetic Couple. By integrating the precision of structural engineering with the nuances of affective computing, I explore how digital mediums act as both conduits for lived sensation and the very armor that shields us from it.

FIG VI. THE MEETING WITH THE REAL

Through this pro-feminist reclamation of the somatic, I challenge the viewer to confront a reality where the digital interface is not a bridge, but a mirror, and where we are never more alone than when we are "together."
